What Sets Us Apart

The Smash Technique

We press every patty onto a 300°C cast iron griddle. The result is a crispy, caramelised crust with a juicy centre. Science never tasted this good.

Locally Sourced

Every vegetable on our menu comes from Kerala farms within 50km. Fresher ingredients, better flavour, and we support the farmers who feed our state.
